Our mission is to enable you to get prepared. The best way to do this is for us to get connected to each other and share resources.

Our manifesto.

We believe in connection, not catastrophe. We know that after a disaster, people come together. UK Emergency is about coming together before a disaster.

  • Who

    UK Emergency was built by SpiralTechnology to encourage people to get connected and get prepared.

  • What

    A people-centered vision of emergency preparedness. Nobody likes to think about emergencies, but UK Emergency takes the mystery out of the process of getting prepared. We do this by providing tools that fit into your daily life.

    UK Emergency is not a new social network. It's a platform designed to plug into your existing networks â€“ whether social or digital.

  • Why

    A little now will mean a lot later. Small actions have a huge impact; connecting with your network, downloading an app, or watching a video is the first step towards being better prepared for an emergency.
