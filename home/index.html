UK Emergency is the UK's hub for emergency preparedness.

  • What is UK Emergency?

    UK Emergency is your hub for emergency preparedness. You’ll find information about what to do in an emergency, simple steps to get connected, and useful guides to help you get prepared. Share this with a friend—and help your loved ones and your local area get prepared.

  • Why 72 Hours?

    In a serious emergency, city services will be impacted, so a basic rule of thumb is for people to be able to take care of each other for 72 hours before help arrives. That’s just three days—think of it as a long weekend—or nine meals.

We believe in connection,
not catastrophe.

Actual emergencies look more like people coming together than cities falling apart. We'll help you to get connected and prepared before an emergency—so we can be that much better off when something happens.
